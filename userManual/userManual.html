1. Getting Started

Welcome to e-commerce platform! This manual will guide you through all the features and functionality available to both customers and administrators.

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Stable internet connection
  • JavaScript enabled in browser settings
Important: This system is designed to work seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices. For the best experience, ensure your browser is updated to the latest version.

2. Customer Guide

2.1 Homepage Access

Your shopping journey begins at our homepage where you can:

  • Navigate to the website homepage without any login requirements
  • Browse featured products
  • View product categories and highlights
  • Access general information about our store
Note: While you can browse products freely, purchasing requires creating an account and logging in for security and order tracking purposes.

2.2 Account Management

Logging In (Existing Users)

  1. Click on the "Login" button
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Sign in" to access your account
  5. You will be redirected to the home page

Creating a New Account (Sign Up)

  1. when on the sign in page, click on the "Sign Up" button located in the bottom
  2. Complete the registration form with the following information:
    • Full name (first and last name)
    • Valid email address
    • Strong password (minimum 8 characters recommended)
    • Confirm password
    • Contact phone number
    • Shipping address
  3. Read and accept the terms of service and privacy policy
  4. Click "Sign up" to complete registration
  5. Check your email for a successful account creation

2.3 Shopping Experience

Accessing Products

  1. After successful login, click on "Shop" in the main navigation menu
  2. Browse through the complete catalog of available products
  3. Products are displayed in a grid layout with:
    • Product images
    • Product names
    • Price information

Search and Filter Functionality

Search and Filter Functionality

  • Locate the search bar at the top of the shop page
  • Enter relevant keywords such as:
    • Product names
  • View filtered results based on your search terms

Sorting Products

Locate the "Sort" dropdown menu and choose from available options:

  • Price: High to Low - Most expensive items first
  • Price: Low to High - Least expensive items first
  • Additional sorting options may include popularity, newest arrivals, or customer ratings

2.4 Product Details and Selection

Viewing Product Details

  1. Click on any product image, name, or "View Details" button
  2. Access comprehensive product information including:
    • High-resolution product images
    • Detailed product description
    • Current price

Adding Products to Cart

  1. Click the "Add to Cart" button
  2. Receive confirmation that the item was successfully added
  3. Choose to continue shopping or proceed to cart

2.5 Cart Management and Checkout

Viewing Your Shopping Cart

  1. Click on the cart icon or "View Cart" button
  2. Review all items in your cart with the following details:
    • Product names and images
    • Selected options (size, color, etc.)
    • Individual quantities
    • Unit prices
    • Line totals
    • Subtotal
    • Estimated taxes
    • Shipping costs
    • Grand total
  3. Make modifications as needed:
    • Update quantities
    • Remove unwanted items
    • Apply discount codes

Checkout Process

  1. From your cart, click "Checkout" or "Proceed to Checkout"
  2. Review and confirm your order summary
  3. Enter or verify shipping information:
    • Delivery address
    • Contact information
    • Special delivery instructions
  4. Select preferred shipping method (if multiple options available)
  5. Review total costs including shipping and taxes
  6. Proceed to payment section

2.6 Payment Process

Our system uses Paynow Sandbox for secure payment processing:

  1. Review final order total
  2. Select payment method
  3. Follow Paynow payment prompts
  4. Complete transaction using provided test payment methods
  5. Receive order confirmation with order number
  6. Get email confirmation with order details
Payment Security: All payment information is processed securely through Paynow's encrypted system. We do not store your payment details on our servers.

3. Administrator Guide

3.1 Admin Login

  1. Access the administrator panel through the designated admin URL
  2. Enter your administrator credentials:
    • Admin username or email
    • Admin password
  3. Click "Login" to access the admin dashboard
  4. Navigate through the admin interface using the sidebar menu
Security Warning: Admin credentials should be kept confidential and changed regularly. Never share admin access with unauthorized personnel.

3.2 Product Management

Adding New Products

  1. Navigate to the "Products" section in the admin panel
  2. Click "Add New Product" or similar button
  3. Complete the product information form:
    • Product name and SKU
    • Detailed description
    • Price and cost information
    • Product category
    • Product images (multiple images recommended)
    • Inventory quantity
    • Product specifications and attributes
    • SEO information (meta description, keywords)
  4. Set product status (active/inactive)
  5. Save the product to make it available for purchase

Editing Existing Products

  1. Go to the product list in the admin panel
  2. Use search or browse to find the product you want to edit
  3. Click the "Edit" button next to the product
  4. Modify necessary information in the product form
  5. Update product images if needed
  6. Adjust inventory levels
  7. Save changes to update the product

Deleting Products

  1. Locate the product in the admin product list
  2. Click the "Delete" button next to the product
  3. Confirm deletion when prompted
Warning: Product deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Consider deactivating products instead of deleting them to preserve order history.

3.3 Order Management

Viewing Orders

  1. Navigate to the "Orders" section in the admin panel
  2. View comprehensive list of all customer orders
  3. Access detailed order information including:
    • Order number and date
    • Customer information and contact details
    • Ordered items with quantities and prices
    • Shipping address
    • Order total and payment status
    • Current order status
  4. Use filters to sort orders by:
    • Date range
    • Order status
    • Customer name
    • Payment status

Managing Order Status

  • Update order status as orders progress:
    • Pending - Order received, awaiting processing
    • Processing - Order being prepared
    • Shipped - Order dispatched to customer
    • Delivered - Order received by customer
    • Cancelled - Order cancelled
  • Add tracking information for shipped orders
  • Include notes for internal reference
  • Communicate order updates to customers via email

4. Payment Information

4.1 Paynow Sandbox Environment

The system currently integrates with Paynow Sandbox for payment processing testing:

  • Purpose: Safe testing environment for payment functionality
  • Test Mode: No real money transactions occur
  • Test Credentials: Use Paynow-provided test payment methods
  • Transaction Simulation: All payment flows can be tested safely

4.2 Production Considerations

For live operations, the following steps are required:

  • Switch from Paynow Sandbox to live Paynow integration
  • Configure live payment credentials
  • Test all payment flows in production environment
  • Implement proper SSL certificates for secure transactions
  • Set up payment reconciliation processes

5. Troubleshooting

5.1 Common Customer Issues

Cannot Purchase Items (Login Required)

Problem: User cannot complete purchase

Solutions:

  • Ensure you are logged in to your account
  • Verify your account email has been confirmed
  • Check that selected items are currently in stock
  • Confirm your shopping cart is not empty
  • Try refreshing the page and logging in again

Login Problems

Problem: Cannot access account

Solutions:

  • Verify email address and password are entered correctly
  • Check if Caps Lock is enabled
  • Use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset your password
  • Clear browser cookies and cache
  • Try using a different browser

Payment Processing Issues

Problem: Payment cannot be completed

Solutions:

  • Ensure you're using valid test credentials in sandbox mode
  • Check your internet connection stability
  • Verify all required payment fields are completed
  • Try refreshing the payment page
  • Contact customer support if problems persist

Search and Filter Issues

Problem: Search not returning expected results

Solutions:

  • Check spelling of search terms
  • Try using broader or alternative keywords
  • Clear all active filters and search again
  • Ensure products you're searching for are in stock

5.2 Common Administrator Issues

Cannot Add or Edit Products

Problem: Product management functions not working

Solutions:

  • Ensure all required fields are completed
  • Check image file formats and sizes meet requirements
  • Verify you have proper administrator permissions
  • Try uploading images one at a time
  • Check for special characters in product names

Orders Not Displaying

Problem: Cannot view customer orders

Solutions:

  • Refresh the admin panel page
  • Check date range filters
  • Verify your admin account has order viewing permissions
  • Clear browser cache and cookies

5.3 Getting Additional Help

If you continue to experience issues after trying the above solutions:

  1. Check your internet connection and browser compatibility
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies completely
  3. Try accessing the system from a different device or browser
  4. Contact the system administrator with specific error details
  5. Report bugs to the development team with screenshots if possible
Support Information: When contacting support, please provide your account email, the steps you were taking when the issue occurred, and any error messages you received.
