1. Getting Started

Welcome to e-commerce platform! This manual will guide you through all the features and functionality available to both customers and administrators.

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Stable internet connection
  • JavaScript enabled in browser settings
Important: This system is designed to work seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices. For the best experience, ensure your browser is updated to the latest version.

2. Customer Guide

2.1 Homepage Access

Your shopping journey begins at our homepage where you can:

  • Navigate to the website homepage without any login requirements
  • Browse featured products
  • View product categories and highlights
  • Access general information about our store
Note: While you can browse products freely, purchasing requires creating an account and logging in for security and order tracking purposes.

2.2 Account Management

Logging In (Existing Users)

  1. Click on the "Login" button
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Sign in" to access your account
  5. You will be redirected to the home page

Creating a New Account (Sign Up)

  1. when on the sign in page, click on the "Sign Up" button located in the bottom
  2. Complete the registration form with the following information:
    • Full name (first and last name)
    • Valid email address
    • Strong password (minimum 8 characters recommended)
    • Confirm password
    • Contact phone number
    • Shipping address
  3. Read and accept the terms of service and privacy policy
  4. Click "Sign up" to complete registration
  5. Check your email for a successful account creation

2.3 Shopping Experience

Accessing Products

  1. After successful login, click on "Shop" in the main navigation menu
  2. Browse through the complete catalog of available products
  3. Products are displayed in a grid layout with:
    • Product images
    • Product names
    • Price information

Search and Filter Functionality

Search and Filter Functionality

  • Locate the search bar at the top of the shop page
  • Enter relevant keywords such as:
    • Product names
  • View filtered results based on your search terms

Sorting Products

Locate the "Sort" dropdown menu and choose from available options:

  • Price: High to Low - Most expensive items first
  • Price: Low to High - Least expensive items first
  • Additional sorting options may include popularity, newest arrivals, or customer ratings

2.4 Product Details and Selection

Viewing Product Details

  1. Click on any product image, name, or "View Details" button
  2. Access comprehensive product information including:
    • High-resolution product images
    • Detailed product description
    • Current price

Adding Products to Cart

  1. Click the "Add to Cart" button
  2. Receive confirmation that the item was successfully added
  3. Choose to continue shopping or proceed to cart

2.5 Cart Management and Checkout

Viewing Your Shopping Cart

  1. Click on the cart icon or "View Cart" button
  2. Review all items in your cart with the following details:
    • Product names and images
    • Individual quantities
    • Unit prices
    • Line totals
    • Subtotal
    • Estimated taxes
    • Shipping costs
    • Grand total
  3. Make modifications as needed:
    • Update quantities
    • Remove unwanted items

Checkout Process

  1. From your cart, click "Checkout" or "Proceed to Checkout"
  2. Proceed to payment section

2.6 Payment Process

Our system uses Paynow Sandbox for secure payment processing:

  1. Review final order total
  2. Select payment method
  3. Follow Paynow payment prompts
  4. Complete transaction using provided test payment methods

3. Administrator Guide

3.1 Admin Login

  1. Access the login page
  2. Enter your administrator credentials:
    • Admin username or email
    • Admin password
  3. Click "Sign up" to access the admin dashboard

3.2 Product Management

Adding New Products

  1. Navigate to the "Products" tab in the admin panel
  2. Click "Add New Product" or similar button
  3. Complete the product information form:
    • Product name
    • Detailed description
    • Price
    • Product category
    • Product images
    • Inventory quantity
  4. Save the product to make it available for purchase

Editing Existing Products

  1. Go to the product grid
  2. Find the product you want to edit
  3. Click the "Edit" button
  4. Modify necessary information in the product form
  5. Update product images if needed
  6. Save changes to update the product

Deleting Products

  1. Locate the product in the admin product grid
  2. Click the "Delete" button next to the product
  3. Confirm deletion when prompted

3.3 Order Management

Viewing Orders

  1. Navigate to the "Orders" tab in the admin panel
  2. View comprehensive list of all customer orders
  3. Access detailed order information including:
    • Order number and date
    • Customer information and contact details
    • Ordered items with quantities and prices
    • Shipping address
    • Order total and payment status
    • Current order status

4. Payment Information

4.1 Paynow Sandbox Environment

The system currently integrates with Paynow Sandbox for payment processing testing:

  • Purpose: Safe testing environment for payment functionality
  • Test Mode: No real money transactions occur
  • Transaction Simulation: All payment flows can be tested safely

This manual covers the current functionality of the e-commerce system.
